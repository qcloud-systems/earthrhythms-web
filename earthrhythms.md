# Earth as a Rhythmic System

The Earth is full of repeating patterns — some fast, some slow, all interconnected.

## Natural Time Signatures
- Tides as **beats**
- Seasons as **phrases**
- Lunar cycles as **time signatures**
- Planetary orbits as **polyrhythms**

## Resonance in Nature
Oceans, atmosphere, and even the Earth’s crust vibrate at characteristic frequencies.

## Rhythm as a Universal Language
From percussion to planetary motion, rhythm is a way of understanding change over time.
